### Textbooks

**1. Error Control Coding**<br/>
BY: S. Lin and D. Costello - 2nd Edition, Prentice-Hall, 2004<br/>
_Comprehensive coverage of finite fields, primitive polynomials, field operations, and their applications in coding theory._

**2. The Theory of Error-Correcting Codes**<br/>
BY: F. MacWilliams and N. Sloane - North-Holland Publishing Company, 1977<br/>
_Classic reference on algebraic coding theory with detailed treatment of finite field arithmetic and algebraic structures._

**3. Fundamentals of Error-Correcting Codes**<br/>
BY: W. Huffman and V. Pless - Cambridge University Press, 2003<br/>
_Accessible introduction to finite fields, polynomial rings, and their role in constructing error-correcting codes._

**4. Introduction to Finite Fields and Their Applications**<br/>
BY: R. Lidl and H. Niederreiter - Cambridge University Press, 1986<br/>
_Comprehensive treatment of finite field theory, minimal polynomials, primitive elements, and practical applications._

**5. A First Course in Coding Theory**<br/>
BY: Raymond Hill - Oxford University Press, 1986<br/>
_Clear explanations of finite field basics including Galois fields and their use in coding theory._

**6. Algebraic Codes for Data Transmission**<br/>
BY: Richard E. Blahut - Cambridge University Press, 2003<br/>
_Detailed mathematical foundations of finite fields with focus on polynomial arithmetic and field extensions._
